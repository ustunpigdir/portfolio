Method

How I evaluate model behavior

The method focuses on finding the exact point where a model’s answer stops being reliable.

01

Controlled prompt design

Prompts are built around one mathematical or linguistic structure, then varied by one constraint at a time.

02

Gold-answer verification

Expected values and reasoning steps are checked before model outputs are evaluated.

03

Error taxonomy

Conceptual errors, numeric errors, response-quality issues, and extraction artifacts are separated.

04

Human review

Automated checks can assist, but final labels require human inspection when reasoning quality matters.

05

Evidence packaging

Reports, README files, cleaned failure logs, and dataset cards turn raw runs into reusable evaluation artifacts.
